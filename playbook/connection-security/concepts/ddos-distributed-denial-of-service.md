# Distributed Denial-of-Service (DDoS)

## Overview

A Distributed Denial-of-Service (DDoS) attack is a DoS attack launched simultaneously from many compromised devices, often organized into a botnet.

## Key Concepts

- Botnet
- Zombies
- Distributed attack
- Bandwidth exhaustion
- Availability

## Why It Matters

DDoS attacks are much harder to stop than traditional DoS attacks because traffic originates from many legitimate-looking systems rather than a single attacker.

## Common Uses

- Website disruption
- Business interruption
- Extortion
- Distraction during larger attacks

## Best Practices

- Use DDoS mitigation services.
- Monitor network traffic continuously.
- Implement rate limiting.
- Develop an incident response plan.
